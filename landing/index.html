What Spectra does

Three layers of signal, one click.

Every profile is read top-down — who they are, how to reach them, and where to send it. Each layer is its own tool, and they share the same evidence.

Analyze

Deterministic scoring runs locally and grades the profile across decision power, founder signal, and hiring intent — each backed by the exact lines of evidence it pulled from the page.

  • Runs in the browser, always
  • Evidence you can click into
  • No profile leaves your machine for scoring

Outreach

Turn the read into a message. Spectra drafts resume-aware outreach that references real signal from the profile — not generic templates — so the first line actually lands.

  • Grounded in extracted evidence
  • Tunes to your résumé & angle
  • Hosted or fully-local generation

Email finder

Surface the most likely work email from name and company patterns, with optional Hunter.io verification when you want a confidence score before you hit send.

  • Pattern-based, instant
  • Optional verification
  • Copy-ready in one tap
How it works

Surface to core, in four steps.

You stay on the profile. Spectra reads what's already on the page.

  1. 01

    Open a profile

    Pin Spectra and click Analyze on any public LinkedIn profile you're viewing.

  2. 02

    Read the signal

    Local scoring grades the profile and shows the evidence behind every number.

  3. 03

    Draft the reach

    Generate outreach that quotes the real signal, tuned to your résumé and goal.

  4. 04

    Find the email

    Surface the likely work email, verify if you want, copy, and send.

Privacy by architecture

The scoring never leaves your browser.

Spectra is local-first by design. Deterministic extraction and scoring always run in the extension. Written insight is the only thing that can touch an LLM — and you choose which one.

Local scoring, always

Signal extraction and grading run entirely in the browser. No profile is sent anywhere to be scored.

You pick the brain

Use the hosted endpoint for zero-setup quality, or point Spectra at a local Ollama model for fully-private generation that never leaves your machine.

No analytics, no tracking

Spectra ships with zero telemetry. Your data stays in local IndexedDB on your device.

No secrets in the client

The hosted proxy holds the API key server-side — nothing sensitive is ever bundled into the extension.

You've reached the core.

Every LinkedIn profile is hiding structure. Spectra surfaces it, grounds your outreach in it, and keeps the whole thing on your machine.
